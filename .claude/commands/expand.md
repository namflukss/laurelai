Deepen `materials/PROFILE.md` with anything that strengthens the festival case. Load the
**festival-strategist** skill (`02-creative-profile.md` for what signals matter).

1. **Re-read** `materials/PROFILE.md` and everything under `materials/` (synopsis, statement,
   reviews, stills, prior submission records).
2. **Search the film's footprint.** Look up the film, the director, and key cast/crew for:
   reviews and press, prior festival selections or laurels, awards, comparable films and where they
   premiered, and any prior premiere that affects eligibility. Fold in anything from `$ARGUMENTS`.
3. **Update the profile.** Enrich the `## Creative read` and add/refresh a `## Track record &
   evidence` section (selections, press quotes with sources, comparables). Update
   `## Completion & premiere status` if you discover a screening that changes premiere eligibility.
4. **Keep it honest.** Cite sources for external claims; never invent a selection, quote, or award.
   Move anything you confirmed out of `## Open questions`.

End with a short note on how the new evidence changes the festival strategy — and suggest re-running
`/plan` if premiere status or the competitive picture shifted.
