\letterhead{Tribeca Festival — New York}{12 June 2026}

Dear Tribeca Programming Team,

I'm writing to submit my feature \textit{The Salt Road} for consideration in the World Narrative
Competition. Tribeca's eye for first features rooted in a specific place is exactly why it's my
opening target: this is a film about a place — a fading copper town — and the people who refuse to
leave it.

\textit{The Salt Road} (94 min, completed 2026) follows a teenage smuggler who inherits her
estranged father's debt and the route that comes with it. It is a world premiere; the film has not
screened publicly, in any form, anywhere.

I'd be glad to provide a secure screener, stills, or any materials your committee needs. Thank you
for your time and for the work Tribeca does for filmmakers like me.

\signoff
